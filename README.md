# 📊 Customer Experience Intelligence Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project presents an **Excel-based Customer Experience Intelligence Dashboard** designed to analyze customer satisfaction (CSAT), sentiment distribution, call trends, and support performance across multiple channels.

The goal is to transform raw customer interaction data into **actionable insights** that improve service quality and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Customer support teams often struggle to answer:

- Why is customer satisfaction dropping?
- Which location or channel is underperforming?
- What are the main drivers of long call durations?
- How does customer sentiment vary across regions?

This dashboard helps stakeholders **identify issues, monitor performance, and optimize customer experience.**

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions Answered

- 📌 Which city has the highest and lowest CSAT score?
- 📊 How is customer sentiment distributed across locations?
- 📈 What are the trends in call volume over time?
- 📉 Which support channel performs best in terms of CSAT?
- ⏱️ Which call reasons lead to longer durations?

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- 🔹 CSAT Tracking (Highest, Lowest, Average)
- 🔹 Sentiment Analysis (Positive, Neutral, Negative)
- 🔹 Call Volume Trend Analysis
- 🔹 Performance by Call Center Location
- 🔹 Channel-wise CSAT Comparison
- 🔹 Call Duration Analysis by Issue Type
- 🔹 Interactive Filters for dynamic insights

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- ✅ **Los Angeles shows the highest CSAT (6.0)** indicating strong service performance  
- ⚠️ **Denver has the lowest CSAT (3.5)** highlighting a critical improvement area  
- 📊 **Call Center channel delivers the highest satisfaction scores**  
- 📉 **Service outages result in the longest call durations**, impacting efficiency  
- 📈 **Call volume spikes indicate potential operational issues or peak demand periods**  
- 😊 **Positive sentiment dominates in high-performing locations**  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- 🚀 Improve service quality in low-performing locations (e.g., Denver)  
- 🔧 Reduce resolution time for service outage issues  
- 📞 Optimize call center operations during peak volume periods  
- 📊 Invest in high-performing channels to scale customer satisfaction  
- 🎯 Monitor sentiment trends to proactively address customer concerns
